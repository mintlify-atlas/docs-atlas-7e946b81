The Executive Dashboard is the home screen of ISOwl. It aggregates data from all modules into a single view so you can assess your organisation's ISO 27001 posture at a glance and identify the highest-priority gaps.

## Dashboard panels

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Global compliance donut" icon="circle-half-stroke">
    Percentage of ISO 27001 Clauses 4–10 requirements marked as **Implemented**. Calculated across all subclauses and requirements in the clause tree.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Annex A maturity donut" icon="shield-check">
    Percentage of applicable ISO 27002:2022 controls marked as **Implemented** out of the 93 controls in Annex A.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Critical alerts" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Live counts for open major nonconformities, open findings, and missing evidence items. Each counter links to the relevant module.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Maturity radar" icon="chart-radar">
    Radar chart plotting the average maturity score (0–5) for each ISO 27001 clause (C4–C10). Hover over each axis to see both maturity level and compliance percentage for that clause.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Clause ranking" icon="bars-progress">
    Horizontal bar chart listing Clauses 4–10 sorted by lowest compliance percentage first. Helps you prioritise which clause needs the most attention.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Top 10 pending requirements" icon="list-check">
    Ordered list of requirements still in **Not started** status. Click any item to jump directly to that requirement in the Clauses module.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Color coding

All percentage indicators on the dashboard follow a consistent traffic-light scheme:

| Range | Color | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| ≥ 80% | Emerald (green) | On track |
| 50–79% | Amber (yellow) | Needs attention |
| < 50% | Rose (red) | Critical gap |

## Exporting the executive report

Click **Reporte Ejecutivo** in the dashboard header to generate a self-contained executive report. The report includes:

- ISMS posture summary with global compliance and Annex A maturity percentages
- Clause-by-clause conformance breakdown with maturity level
- Domain progress breakdown (Annex A control domains)
- Open findings summary
- Critical findings highlight (NC Mayor only)

The PDF downloads directly to your browser's default download folder. No data leaves your machine.

<Warning>
  ISOwl stores all data in `localStorage`. PDF export is the only persistent backup mechanism. Export regularly to preserve a record of your ISMS state.
</Warning>

## How scores are calculated

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Global compliance (Clauses 4–10)">
    Counts the total number of requirements across all clauses (4 through 10). Requirements marked as **Implementado** or **No aplica** are both counted as compliant. The percentage is `(Implementado + No aplica) / total requirements × 100`, rounded to the nearest integer.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Annex A maturity score">
    Counts controls with status **Implementado** divided by the total number of controls that are not marked **No Aplica** (i.e. applicable controls only). Marking more controls as **No Aplica** shrinks the denominator and can raise the percentage without adding new implementations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Maturity radar">
    Averages the individual maturity slider values (0–5) across all requirements within each ISO 27001 clause (4 through 10). Each axis on the radar represents one clause. Clauses with no evaluated requirements show a score of 0. The overall average maturity is shown in the top-right corner of the radar panel.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Clause ranking order">
    Clauses are sorted ascending by their compliance percentage so the lowest-performing clause always appears at the top of the list.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
