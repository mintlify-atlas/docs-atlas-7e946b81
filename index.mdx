## What is ISOwl?

ISOwl is a full-stack ISO 27001 GRC (Governance, Risk & Compliance) dashboard designed for information security professionals, CISOs, and GRC consulting agencies. It provides a single platform to implement, manage, and demonstrate compliance with the ISO 27001:2022 Information Security Management System standard.

Whether you're managing your own organization's ISMS or running a GRC consulting firm overseeing multiple clients, ISOwl gives you the tools to track requirements, assess risks, collect evidence, manage audits, and export professional compliance reports.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the application">
    Launch ISOwl in your browser. You'll be greeted by the login screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Use your credentials to log in. For agency admins: `admin@agencia.com` / `admin123`. For client owners: `owner@cliente.com` / `cliente123`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the dashboard">
    The executive dashboard gives you an immediate overview of global compliance, Annex A maturity, and critical alerts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start tracking requirements">
    Navigate to **Clauses 4–10** to begin marking ISO 27001 requirements as in-progress or implemented.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assess your controls">
    Go to **Annex A** to evaluate all 93 ISO 27002:2022 controls for your organization's Statement of Applicability.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  ISOwl stores all data locally in your browser using `localStorage`. No backend server is required — your compliance data stays in your environment.
</Note>
